About FurniCraft

Transforming houses into homes since 2020

Our Story

FurniCraft was founded with a simple mission: to make premium furniture accessible to everyone. We believe that everyone deserves to have a beautiful, comfortable home without breaking the bank.

What started as a small showroom has grown into a thriving e-commerce platform, serving thousands of happy customers across the country. Our passion for quality furniture and exceptional customer service drives everything we do.

Today, we offer a curated selection of furniture pieces that combine style, comfort, and durability. Every item in our collection is carefully chosen to meet our high standards and exceed our customers' expectations.

Our Journey

2020 - The Beginning

Founded FurniCraft with a vision to revolutionize furniture shopping. Started with a small showroom and 50 products.

2021 - Going Digital

Launched our online store and expanded our product catalog to over 500 items. Reached 10,000 satisfied customers.

2022 - Rapid Growth

Opened two new showrooms and expanded delivery across India. Celebrated 50,000 customers milestone.

2023 - Excellence Recognition

Won "Best Furniture E-commerce Platform" award. Surpassed 100,000 happy customers and 1,000+ 5-star reviews.

2024 - Continued Innovation

Launched a new AR product preview feature and expanded service to more Indian cities. Current focus on sustainability initiatives.
